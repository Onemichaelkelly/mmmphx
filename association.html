Our Mission

We're working to make McDowell Road safer and more walkable — with protected bike lanes, tree-lined barriers, and median traffic calming between 7th and 20th Streets.

We champion existing businesses, support new ones through the revitalization committee, and celebrate the diverse community that makes the Mile unlike anywhere else in Phoenix.

Founded formally in 2014, the MMMA grew from the grassroots energy of business owners who saw the Mile's potential and decided to organize around it.
