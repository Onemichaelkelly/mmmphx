Events on the Mile

Community celebrations, holiday markets, merchant mixers, and more.

Recurring Annual
Signature Event

Miracle Mile Days

The revival of the original 1950s celebration. Live music, food, local vendors, and community pride along the Mile. First held again in November 2019.

Holiday Season
Shopping

Mercado on Miracle Mile

Holiday shopping event with pop-up shops, live music, food vendors, and street light displays. 3-7 PM on McDowell from 14th to 20th Streets.
