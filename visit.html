Plan Your Visit

Spend a Day on the Mile

Whether you're here for an hour or a full day, the Mile has something for everyone.

☕

Morning

Start at Froth Coffee Roasters for locally roasted coffee and Noble Bakery pastries. Walk the Mile and browse ShapeShifter's mid-century furniture collection.

🍴

Afternoon

Lunch at Restaurante el Guanaquito for authentic Salvadoran pupusas. Explore Jungle Box's rare plant collection. Find Arizona treasures at Eyeconic Arizona.

🍺

Evening

Craft beers at Wilderness Miracle Mile — try the Miracle Mild flagship. Catch live music or a pop-up event. End with the Gateway Arch glowing under the lights.

Find Us on McDowell Road

The Miracle Mile stretches along East McDowell Road from roughly 7th Street to SR-51 in central Phoenix.

Getting There

East McDowell Road between 7th St and SR-51 in central Phoenix. Take the McDowell exit from SR-51, or head east on McDowell from downtown.

Parking

Free street parking is available along McDowell Road and side streets throughout the district. Most businesses also have their own parking lots.

Public Transit

Valley Metro bus routes serve McDowell Road with stops throughout the Miracle Mile corridor. The light rail connects at nearby stations.

Photo Spots

The Gateway Arch at golden hour. The "Cultura" mural by GraffAholeks at 1612 N. 16th St. Vintage neon signage throughout the corridor.

Best Times to Visit

Mornings for coffee and browsing. Evenings for dining and craft beer. Weekends for the best chance of catching pop-up events and live music.

Stay Nearby

FOUND:RE Phoenix Hotel at 1100 N. Central Ave. offers boutique gallery-style rooms just minutes from the Mile.
